Nesqual is more than tech company

We are a team of ten highly skilled consultants and developers who specialize in Okta, the leading identity and access management platform. With our extensive knowledge and expertise, we help businesses of all sizes to implement, configure, and optimize their Okta solutions. Our team consists of Okta Certified Consultants and Developers who have a deep understanding of the intricacies of IAM systems. We have worked with clients across a range of industries, from healthcare and finance to government and education. Our experience enables us to offer a comprehensive suite of services, including IAM strategy development, implementation, customization, and ongoing support.
